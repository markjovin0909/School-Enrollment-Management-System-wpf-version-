## Signoff Gates
1. Scope Integrity
- [ ] All tickets are within Step 1 frozen scope or explicitly marked structural.
- [ ] No hidden out-of-scope feature creep in Quick Wins.

2. Governance Integrity
- [ ] Tickets trace to Step 2 flows and Step 4 risk controls.
- [ ] High-risk actions have explicit backlog coverage.

3. Dependency Integrity
- [ ] Critical dependency chains are explicit (QW -> ME -> SR).
- [ ] No ticket starts before governance dependencies are complete.

4. Acceptance Integrity
- [ ] Every ticket has measurable acceptance criteria.
- [ ] Definition of done is testable and auditable.

5. Delivery Integrity
- [ ] Phase sequencing is realistic for team bandwidth.
- [ ] Owners are assigned before execution begins.
