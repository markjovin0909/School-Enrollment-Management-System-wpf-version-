## Signoff Gates
1. Scope and Sequencing
- [ ] Sprint board covers all Step 5 tickets.
- [ ] Dependencies are honored in sequence.
- [ ] P0 governance tickets are front-loaded.

2. Delivery Feasibility
- [ ] Estimated effort per sprint matches team capacity.
- [ ] Owner assignments are realistic.
- [ ] Release gates are testable.

3. Governance Feasibility
- [ ] Critical controls (reason code, preflight, audit completeness) are delivered before structural features.
- [ ] Tier3 operation controls are release-gated.

4. Quality Feasibility
- [ ] QA focus areas are defined per sprint wave.
- [ ] Regression stop conditions are approved.

5. KPI Feasibility
- [ ] Baseline comparison points are scheduled (S2, S5, S8).
- [ ] KPI thresholds for go/no-go are agreed.
